# Yield Structure

There are two ways in which depositors can accrue yield:

1. All deposits into the RWI Vault earn a fixed [Baseline Yield](/baseline-yield) that accrues programmatically over time and is protected by [Nexus Mutual cover](/baseline-yield/nexus-mutual-cover).
2. Additional [bonuses](/bonuses) may be earned by locking deposits for longer periods, allowing depositors to participate in excess returns generated by the underlying insurance business.
